Features

This Isn't Another Test App

Confiddo helps you practice, learn from hints, reattempt without penalty, and watch your confidence grow over time.

Hints, Not Answers

Stuck on a question? Use hints to learn the approach. Every hint teaches a concept, not just gives an answer.

Reattempt Without Penalty

Got it wrong? Try again. Every attempt makes you stronger. No scores, no judgment — just learning.

Watch Yourself Grow

Track your streaks, earn stars, collect characters. See your confidence grow — compared only to yourself.

Effort Over Marks

Teachers and parents see your effort and growth — never your mistakes. They're here to support you.

Smart Notifications

Push notifications keep everyone in the loop. New tests, deadlines, results — right on your phone.

Safe & Private

Student data stays protected. No public leaderboards, no peer comparison. A safe space to learn.

Our Approach

Practice That Feels Different

We believe confidence comes from effort, not comparison. Here's what makes Confiddo different.

🛡️

No Public Scores

We don't display marks or rankings. Students see their own growth trajectory — never compared to classmates. Learning happens best without the fear of being judged.

🌱

Growth Over Grades

Our three pillars — Readiness, Growth, and Confidence — measure how a student develops over time. It's not about one test; it's about the journey.

🤝

A Team Effort

Students, teachers, parents, and principals — all on one platform, all working toward the same goal: helping every child believe in themselves.
